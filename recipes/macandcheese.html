Description

Classic homemade mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni (or your preferred pasta)
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or a mix of your choice)
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Cheese Sauce
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce
  4. Bake (Optional)
  5. Serve and Enjoy
